    About Us

    Background

    S.C.S is located in Nasuti North village, Nasuti Parish, Nambale Subcounty, Iganga District approximately 18km from Iganga town along kaliro Road. The school currently has a population of 270 pupils and majority in the boarding section.


    Foundation

    S.C.S was founded in 2008 by Bishop David Byangu. The major aim was to expand the provision of education to the children within and beyond the community.

    Frequently Asked Questions FAQs

    Below are some of the frequently asked questions and their respective answers.

    • 01 Does your school admit Christian children only?

      Not really! Despite the fact that our school has a Christian foundation, we admit pupils of all religions.

    • 02 Does your school teach following the standard curriculum?

      We teach following the guidelines provided by the Ministry of Education and Sports (MOES) and the National Curriculum Development Centre(NCDC).

    • 03 Will my child's health be maintained at your school?

      At our school, we observe the Standard Operating Procedures (S.O.Ps) as instructed by the Ministry of Health (MOH)
